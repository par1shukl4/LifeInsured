About Us

In the 21st century, personalized healthcare and financial security have become paramount as individuals face increasingly complex health risks and lifestyle challenges. However, the current landscape of medical insurance remains largely generic, failing to account for diverse personal health metrics and lifestyle choices. Consumers, therefore, struggle to find insurance plans that align precisely with their unique health conditions, habits, and financial needs.


Life Insured seeks to address this gap by creating a comprehensive, consumer-focused database management system that bridges the divide between consumers and their most suitable health insurance plans. Leveraging health data—such as heart rates, smoking habits, BMI, and exercise frequency—this platform offers a nuanced approach to insurance matching that considers both lifestyle and medical history, optimizing the likelihood of coverage that is both affordable and truly relevant to individual health profiles.


Objective: To develop a database management system that dynamically connects consumers with tailored health insurance options, promoting personalized, accessible healthcare coverage. By focusing on health metrics and lifestyle habits, Life Insured not only empowers consumers to make informed insurance choices but also aligns with the global shift toward preventive healthcare, equitable access, and financial inclusion as defined in the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being and SDG 10: Reduced Inequality).
